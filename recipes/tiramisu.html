Tiramisu

Description

Easy and refreshing dessert ready in 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 500g ladyfinger
  • 800g mascarpone
  • 8 eggs
  • 500ml espresso
  • 100ml Marsala wine
  • 200ml Hazelnut liquor
  • 50g cocoa powder
  • 100g demerara sugar

Steps:

  1. Whisk the sugar with the egg yolks
  2. Add the mascarpone and mix
  3. Add the Marsala wine
  4. Whisk the egg whites separate and incorporate into the mascarpone mix
  5. Combine the espresso with the Hazelnut liquor
  6. Layer the dipped ladyfingers then add the mascarpone mix
  7. Repeat till u filled the whole tray
  8. At the end dust with the cocoa powder
