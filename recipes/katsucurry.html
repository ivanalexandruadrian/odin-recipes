Katsu curry

Description:

Tasty, heartwarming and comforting curry thats very easy to make.

Serve it with steamed sushi rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 onions
  • 3 carrots
  • 400g potatoes
  • 400g chicken thighs or breast
  • 3 curry blocks
  • 1/2 apple
  • 1l chicken stock
  • 1 bunch of coriander leaves
  • a few tablespoon coconut oil

Steps:

  1. Cook the diced chicken in coconut oil
  2. Add the onion and the carrots
  3. Grate de apple and add it to the pot
  4. After a few minutes add the chicken stock
  5. When it starts boiling add the chopped potatoes
  6. Cook for 20 minutes then finish with fresh coriander
