Meatballs soup

Description

Grandma recipe of my favourite soup.Traditional and very popular in my country.It can be made with pork, beef or chicken mince.Good for the days after u had a night out.

Serve it with a dollop of sour cream/creme fraiche and a fresh hot pepper

Ingredients:

  • 500g minced meat
  • 1kg beef/pork bones
  • 2 medium onion
  • 2/3 carrots
  • 100g celeriac
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 small parsnip
  • 3 medium tomatoes or 200g canned tomatoes
  • 1 litre of borsch(if u cant get it substitute with lemon juice or vinegar but babushka is never gonna forgive you)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • handfull of rice
  • salt,pepper and paprika powder
  • a bunch of parsley/lovage/dill

Steps:

  1. Add 3 litres of water along with the bones and bring them to simmer for 30 minutes
  2. Dice one of the onions along with the carrots,parsnip and celeriac
  3. Fry the veggies in a little oil for 4/5 minutes and add them to the pot
  4. Add the diced red bell pepper and the tomatoes
  5. Prepare the meatballs by mixing it with the onion, rice, egg, thyme, salt, pepper and paprika
  6. Add the meatballs and let them simmer for 10 minutes
  7. Add the borsch and cook for another 10 minutes
  8. Remove the bones from the soup
  9. Finish with the parsley, lovage or both and season with salt and pepper
